Yatzy dice game & score manager for Android!

by Rasmus Puls

Posted on Febuary 09, 2017 at 9:00 PM

As part in my android yatzy app I developed a dice rolling game!

What is a yatzy score manager app without a little sidekick of a dice rolling game? What if you lose a die doen a sewer drain in the middle of a game? Yes I fired up unity to build my very own dice rolling game for yatzy, and I wanted to make it special by using simulated physics to roll the dices. In blender I created a die model & a cup which i both imported to unity and made prefabs of. I added a special mesh collider to the cup that would keep the dices from falling through the sides and bottom, since mesh colliders are expensive and "bad practice" due to their level of complextity I came up with my own idea. I went inside sketchup and created a very simplyfied version of the cup and sized it to fit inside between the outer and inner walls of the graphical cup, I then used that model to generate a much more simple mesh collider and positioned that model inside the graphical model so it is not visible at any time.



Anything else it does?
