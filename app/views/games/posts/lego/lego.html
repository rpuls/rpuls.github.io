"Legokart" - greenfoot mini game

by Rasmus Puls

Posted on Febuary 10, 2017 at 11:21 AM

Greenfoot game development framework is nice and easy, but very limited compared to unity!

This was a project I did on a "cross class" school project with service management students, controller students, graphical design students. They demanded a minigame from me, and our main topic was "legoland".. I came up with this tiny game created in greenfoot and taken inspiration by the old danish web game "Mujaffa"
